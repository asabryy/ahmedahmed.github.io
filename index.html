Who Am I?

Hi I'm Ahmed Sabry and I am a Software Developer. I had my first coding course in high school and built my first program (a calculator written in Pascal) in grade 9. My professional interests lie in Software Development, Machine Learning, IT, Automotive and Game Development. I recently graduated from the University of Victoria with a Bachelor's Degree in Computer Science and am eager to develop and apply my knowledge/skills in the software industry.
