About Me

Hi, I’m Ahmed — a data-driven problem solver with a background in computer science and a growing passion for analytics.

I completed my undergraduate degree in Computer Science at the University of Victoria, where I became especially interested in data science and machine learning. While much of my exposure to these fields came through academic projects, I’ve always been drawn to the way data can tell stories and drive better decisions.

Over the past two years, I’ve worked in the healthcare IT industry, supporting system infrastructure, managing endpoint deployments, and automating workflows in high-stakes environments. That experience taught me how valuable good data and clean reporting can be—especially when it comes to improving efficiency and supporting critical operations.

Now, I’m transitioning into data analytics, combining my technical strengths in Python and SQL with tools like Power BI and Excel to explore real-world datasets, build dashboards, and uncover insights. I’ve completed the Google Data Analytics Certificate and am preparing for the Microsoft Power BI certification (PL-300).

Outside of work, you’ll probably find me analyzing esports stats, diving into new tech, or chasing the perfect shawarma.
